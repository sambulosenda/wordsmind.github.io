WordsMind

WordsMind is the Eddictive Infinite Word Search Puzzle. Start finding words!

Find words up, down, left, right, diagonal, and backwards! Amazing every day you will find a different puzzle. The puzzle will have the most important events that happened on that date. The list of the hidden words is provided, When you pass a lot of levels. The puzzle game have a theme to which all the hidden words are related.
